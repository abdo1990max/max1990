Programming and robotics as a tool of evil

Programming and robotics are powerful tools that can bring about tremendous positive change in the world, but they also carry risks if used irresponsibly. The future of this technology depends on how humans handle it and how well we balance technological progress with ethical values. If used wisely, robots can be a powerful force for good, but if misused, they can become a source of evil and destruction.
